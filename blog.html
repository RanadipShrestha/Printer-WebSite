Technology Introduction

Technology is science or knowledge which makes our work more easier and comfortable. Technology has played a vital role to change our lives in countless ways by revolutionizing how we work, live and play. Technology has its own advantages and disadvantages.


Some of the positive impacts are:

Communication: With the help of technology, we are able to communicate to the person who are located seven oceans far also with the one clicks on the technology products.


Education: With the helps of technology, we are able to have the quality education without any physical appearances. We are able to learn different things with the help of technology which is not also mentioned in the class, school, college, etc. With the help of technology, we are able to get the education when the world is suffering from the covid pandemic.


Health: With the help of different technology, the healthcare industry has advancements in identifying health problems early. We are getting the knowledge to improve our health just by using the technology.


Productivity: Technology has vastly increased the productivity, since the ability of computers to solve complex problem in just less than seconds which helps us to be more productive by saving our times.


Some of the negative impacts are:

Slouched Posture: Using the technology product like smartphone are hurting our posture. Continuously using of smartphones is giving the pain in the neck. A slouched posture can reflect our emotions like being sad, scared, or feeling powerless or depressed. The relevance here is it seems that bad posture cannot only reflect a negative state of mind but can also cause us to think negatively.


Poor Sleep: Better sleep has numerous benefits, including our state of mind and mood. The quality of our sleep can be negatively affected by tech items.


Social Media Influence: Social media can bring happiness or sadness in our life. People who spent more time on social media are missing from their friends and family circle.
